Hi all
 
Just to let you know that TD is now CTCSS only – (no 1750hz tone access and no carrier only).  Apart from that all is as usual.
 
We have taken this step to try and overcome the 1 second pulsing and other QRM from keeping the repeater open for long periods.
 
From the tests Andy, G0BEQ and myself have carried out this morning there doesn’t seem to too many issues.
 
We still have the squelch operating – we did try open squelch but there was a bit of a squelch tail on it.
 
Any reports to me please.
 
Rob, G4XUT
